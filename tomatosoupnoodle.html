Tomato soup noodle

Ingredients

  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 stick of spring Onion
  • Flour
  • Hard tofu cut into small bites
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt and Pepper
  1. Cut tomatoes into small chunks, the smaller the better.
  2. Cut white part of spring onions off and into half.
  3. Prepare flour noodles. Mix flour with water until it becomes a thick consistency but liquid enough to pour, a liquid pancake batter if you like.
  4. Once flour batter is the right consistency, pour enough water on top to cover batter.
  5. Heat pot, pour oil and stir fry root of spring onion for a few minutes.
  6. Add in chopped up tomatoes and cook until tomatoes is soft and well cooked through.
  7. Season with soy sauce, salt and pepper and cook for about a minute.
  8. Pour in hot water to about the right amount, not too thin or too thick.
  9. Pour out water slowly from the flour batter and use chopstick to vigorously mix batter till the side comes off easily.
  10. Turn heat up on pot, slowly take the flour batter and with a quick motion peel batter off the side while pouring out, do this continously until you make noodles into the pot.
  11. Cook soup until all the noodles are cooked through, add tofu.
  12. Serve with a few drops of sesame oil on top.
