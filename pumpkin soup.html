Pumpkin soup

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin half
  • Water
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • Milk or cream
  1. Remove core and seeds of pumpkin and remove outer skin of pumpkin.
  2. Cut pumpkin into smaller chunks.
  3. Put pumpkin into a big pot and fill up with water but not too much, about half of pumpkin.
  4. Cook pumpkin, covered, until soft, about 20 minutes.
  5. Once pumpkin is soft, mash pumpkin with a fork or wooden spoon.
  6. If not enough liquid, add water to your liking.
  7. Season with salt and pepper, and add a dash of cream last.
  8. Serve with hot toast.
